Banking Wallet App

Bank Wallet app is designed to cater specifically to the financial needs of South African citizens, offering a comprehensive set of features to simplify their daily transactions. Developed using Flutter, this app combines user-friendly design with robust functionality, providing a seamless and secure financial experience.

Key Features:

  • Add Balance in Wallet: South African citizens can easily load funds into their wallet directly from their bank accounts. This feature ensures quick access to available funds for various transactions.
  • Top-Up: Users can conveniently top up their mobile phones, ensuring uninterrupted connectivity. Whether it's airtime or data, our app simplifies the process.
  • Bill Payment: Paying bills is hassle-free with our app. Users can settle their utility bills, such as electricity, water, and internet, directly from their wallet, eliminating the need for multiple transactions.
  • Recharge: In addition to topping up mobile phones, our app allows users to recharge various prepaid services, including electricity meters and public transportation cards.
  • QR Payments: Embracing modern payment methods, our app supports QR code payments. Users can make secure and convenient payments at participating merchants by simply scanning QR codes.

Development and Team:

  • Flutter Framework: The app is developed using the Flutter framework, ensuring a smooth and consistent user experience across Android and iOS platforms.
  • Team Size: Our dedicated team of three professionals collaboratively worked on this project, with me serving as the primary developer.
  • State Management: We implemented the Provider-Consumer state management pattern to efficiently manage and update the app's state, ensuring a responsive and intuitive user interface.
  • Task Management: GitLab served as our task management tool, facilitating code version control, issue tracking, and collaborative development.
